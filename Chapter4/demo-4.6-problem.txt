Taco shop has 3 different types of tacos
each the same price.

On tuesday tacos are only 1.00 
every other day they are 1.50.

A customer can order a combo box which means
they get 9 tacos at .99 each, regardless of what 
day it is. 

Your task is come up with a function to calculate
the cost of an order based on the quantity of tacos
ordered and what day of the week it is.

Come up with a data definition to capture 
the information in the problem.
